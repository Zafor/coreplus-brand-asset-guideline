Tone Of Voice

Each element of the our vision is tied back into the core message of ‘Practice Happy’, to align aspects of the brand and evoke emotion. Our promise is consistent with the voice of our brand and the communication tone is key when we are building a bridge with our customers. Single-mindedness is a powerful lever in communicating a clear message to customers, which is absorbed through simplicity and repetition.

Writing Style

Our writing style is focused on communicating for four different purposes. They are

  1. New User
  2. Existing User
  3. Plus Elements
  4. Core
New User
  • Starting your online health practice is simple & cost-effective with coreplus. Take the next step today.
  • Have things changed? Create your own health practice online and manage it with simplicity and effectiveness.
Existing User
  • Too much admin? coreplus helps you streamline your healthcare practice saving time, frustration & money. Switch to coreplus today.
  • Maximise your growth potential with a digital health practice tool that has all the key features to scale your healthcare business at a fraction of the cost.
Plus Elements
  • Medicare cash flows faster with coreplus’s integrated Online Claiming technology
  • Connecting, helping and growing thousands of health professionals using coreplus.
Core
  • Practice Happy: use Australia’s leading online Digital Health practice management software as a service, any time, from anywhere, with any device and internet browser.
  • For the modern health professional seeking quality patient & practice management software accessible anywhere, anytime.

Logo

The logo is a composite of an origami-styled multicolored triangular symbol and the words “core” and “plus” concatenated into one word “coreplus” with the tagline “practice happy”, depicted with the tagline “practice happy”, the words “core” and “practice happy” are in light grey and the word “plus” is in dark grey.

The Extensions of the Logo

The origami pyramid as a symbol demonstrates the possibilities to the user in working in the “core” of the product, being the patient and practice management, and then extending the functionality to suit their practice needs and taking advantage of the “plus” elements of coreplus. The user becomes the origami artist, changing the shape of coreplus to suit their health practice needs whilst retaining a solid and strong patient and practice management core.

The origami pyramid is comprised of eight (8) equilateral triangles of diverse colors, four (4) of which are placed to form a larger equilateral triangle, three (3) of which connect at the points of the larger triangle and one (1) which is placed at the bottom right of the larger triangle, depicting an equilateral triangle folded toward the viewer or positioned on a different plane to the other triangles.

It is designed to communicate that coreplus is a patient/ practice management solution that extends functions and workflow into four key areas i.e. cashflow optimisation, online add-ons, referrals and eHealth by using complementary, attractive yet different colours.

Minimum Clearance Space

Minimum size

The minimum size the Coreplus logo may be reproduced is 45mm.

Clear space

To ensure the Coreplus logo is not compromised by other graphic devices, use the minimum clear space guidelines. Use the height of a full triangle (marked ‘X’) as a proportional measure. No other elements should breach this area.
